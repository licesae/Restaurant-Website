about us

Welcome to Koga Cafe — your cozy corner for comfort, coffee, and community...tucked away in a charming spot and open daily from 8am to 5pm, we’re here to make your mornings brighter and your afternoons more peaceful. At Koga, we believe in slow mornings, hearty breakfasts, and the simple joy of a perfectly brewed cup of coffee. Whether you're dropping by for a quick bite or settling in with a book and a latte, our space is designed to feel like home — warm, welcoming, and always full of good vibes. Come for the pancakes, stay for the peace.
